BIOGRAPHY

Brooklynzhen, the musical alias of Glasgow-based artist Allan McCafferty, is a versatile musician, producer, and developer. With a background in death-metal and drum machines, Brooklynzhen has transitioned into the realms of underground house and techno, drawing inspiration from influential figures such as Sub club DJs, Homework by Daft Punk, and the French underground house scene.

In a world where diversity and division coexist, Brooklynzhen's music aims to create moments and memories that uplift spirits, making underground, grimey house music the sanctuary of happiness.

Brooklynzhen's debut single, "33rd Degree Jason," offers a unique blend of underground house and techno with a traditional song structure, making it easily accessible to a wide range of audiences, including various counter-cultures. Recorded using an old refurbished Roland SH101 and a MacBook Pro, the track stands out for its innovative approach to engaging listeners with its house and techno elements.

Keep an eye out for Brooklynzhen's upcoming live and DJ sets post-summer 2025, showcasing the artist's dynamic evolution and commitment to minimalist yet impactful music production.
